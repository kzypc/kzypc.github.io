The 5 Best Home Workout For Men Without Equipment

Build Muscle | Lose Weight | More Confident

Posted by Khor Ze Yi on April 8, 2024

Home workout brings ton of benefits as it help you to build muscle, lose weight and more confidence. If you are a men and you wish to workout at home witout equipment, this is the right place for you. Below are showing the 5 best home workout for men without equipment for your home workout routine, including lunges, push ups, burpees, planks and squats.

  1. Home Workout Routine - The Lunges

    2. 10 reps * 5 sets

    Image from JEFIT

    Steps to do push ups are:

    1. Start by standing up straight
    2. Step foward with left leg and squat down through hips, driving the whole body toward ground and feel your the squeeze from your quads muscle
    3. Hold for 5 seconds and move the leg from behind foward.
    4. Do it one more time with opposite side, and consider one rep. 10 reps as 1 set, do it 5 sets.

    Lunges mainly train the upper legs muscles (quads and hamstring), glutes and calf muscles.

    Based on article from Voza, lunges is a high intensity workout as a person weight around 68KG doing lunges for 30 minutes will burn round 275 calories and train cardiovascular muscle as well.

  2. Home Workout Routine - The Push Ups

    3. 10 reps * 5 sets

    Image from JEFIT

    Steps to do lunges are:

    1. Start by lying face down on the floor, as arms and legs maintain at shoulder width.
    2. Slowly straighten your arms and push the whole body off the floor. Your legs, hips and head should be a straight line only arm is moving.
    3. Keep your core tight and feel the squeeze of your chest muscles.
    4. 10 reps as 1 set, do it 5 sets.

    Based on the article from Preiato, the main muscles push up train are chest(Pectoralis major and Pectoralis minor), triceps and core muscles. The benefits of push up are strengthen upper body, reduce the cardiac events risk and imporve body composition.

  3. Home Workout Routine - The Burpees

    4. 10 reps * 5 sets

    Image from Men'sHealth

    Steps to do burpees are:

    1. Stand with your feet about twice of your shoulder-width and arms just naturally place at your sides
    2. Squat down until your hand can touch the ground between your legs
    3. Keep your core and upper body tight and kick your legs backward. Land it on the ground as your leg is strenghten.
    4. Push with your arm and leg as your body is air, pull your legs back to the original position and then jump into the air
    5. After the 4 steps, consider 1 reps. 10 reps as 1 set, do it 5 sets

    Article from James Roland mentioned that burpee targeted all the muscles of human. It burn 375 calories every 30 minutes of burpees, which is a more intensive exercise than lunges

  4. Home Workout Routine - The Plank

    5. 30 seconds * 5 sets

    Image from JEFIT

    Steps to do planks are:

    1. Start by lying face down on the floor, as arms and legs maintain at shoulder width.
    2. Keep your core tight and raise your body up into the air. At this time the only body part touching the tround is your feet and your forearms
    3. Your feet, hips and head should keep in a straight line.
    4. Keep this position for 30 seconds, consider one rep. 10 reps as 1 set, do it 5 sets.

    Based on the article from peaky96, planks can train back muscles and core muscles isometrically, creating a more balance body strength.

    If your are interested in abdominals workout remember to visit the article of The Ultimate Ab Workout for more information.

  5. Home Workout Routine - Squat

    6. 10 reps * 5 sets

    Image from JEFIT

    Steps to do squat are:

    1. Start by standing up, placing your feet about shoulder-width
    2. Bend your knee, sitting down with your hips
    3. Hold your hip in the air for 5 seconds
    4. Push your feet to the ground, and back to the original position
    5. After the above four steps you have done one rep. 10 reps as 1 set, do it 5 sets.

    According to the article from Sara Lindberg squat exercise targeted your quads, hamstrings and glutes muscles. It is a basic exercise but extremely beneficial for everyone who done this. Benefit of squat including strengthen core, strengthen lower body and burn 223 calories for every 30 minutes squat exercise.
